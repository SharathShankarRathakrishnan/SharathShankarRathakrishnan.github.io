About Me

Education

I finished my Bachelor's in Electronics and Communication Engineering at Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai and then moved to the United States for my Master's in Computer Science at New Jersey Institute of Technology.

Switching fields was a challenge, but it allowed me to move away from hardware and focus entirely on the software and systems I actually enjoy building.

Originated From

I was born and raised in Chennai, a city on the South Indian coast known for its non-stop energy and some of the best food you'll ever eat.

I'm proud of my Tamil heritage, which comes with a language and a history that's thousands of years old.

Growing up in a place with that much soul taught me how to find my own path in a busy world.

Currently At

I moved to the United States to pursue my Master's in Computer Science at NJIT and I'm currently living in Harrison, New Jersey.

It's a completely different pace of life compared to the chaos of home, but being here has been a great way to sharpen my skills and see the tech world from a fresh perspective while I focus on my studies and personal projects.

Coding

I've always had a natural knack for seeing the "signal" in the middle of a lot of noise.

That's why Machine Learning clicked for me. It's not just about the code, it's about having the intuition to spot patterns that most people miss.

I actually have a love-hate relationship with messy datasets where I usually hate the cleanup, but the dopamine hit of a perfectly functioning pipeline makes the grind worth it every time.

Hobby

I celebrate a long day of staring at code by staring at video games, because apparently, I just really enjoy looking at glowing rectangles.

But whenever the weather is actually nice enough to be outside, I'll trade the mechanical keyboard for a tennis racket and some actual sunlight.
